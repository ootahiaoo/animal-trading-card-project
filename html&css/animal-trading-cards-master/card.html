Pig

Pigs communicate with each other using 20 different recognizable sounds.

  • Scientific Name: Sus scrofa domesticus
  • Average Length: 1.5 m
  • Average Lifespan: 15 years
  • Habitat: Every continent except Antartica

Although they might differ in shape, size and color, pigs have a distinctive round nose, and four feet with four toes. They are omnivore and have three times more taste buds than humans. They are social animals that live in herds led by females.
